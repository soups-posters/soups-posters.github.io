How does it work?

  1. Explore the posters
  2. Join the Zoom Meeting (Invite link can be found in #soups21-posters in the SOUPS Slack)
  3. Select the Breakout Room of the poster you would like to visit
  4. Authors have shared their names and pronouns they would prefer you to use, please respect these
  5. Be excellent to each other
